✦ About Me

The person
behind the pixels.

I'm a UX researcher and designer with 4 years of experience at Spring Education Group, where I was the first UX hire and built the research practice from zero inside a 10,000+ person company.

Research is where I spend most of my time. User interviews, card sorting, usability testing, Hotjar recordings, GA4 funnels, A/B tests. I use whatever it takes to understand what's actually happening and why. The design work comes after that, and it's better for it.

Outside of work I'm usually hiking with my two corgis, playing pickleball, or headbanging at a concert somewhere.
