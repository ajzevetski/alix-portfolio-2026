✦ About Me

The person
behind the pixels.

I'm a Senior UX Designer with 4 years of experience designing and launching web products in fast-moving environments. I was the founding UX hire at Spring Education Group, where I built the UX practice from scratch inside a 10,000+ person org — defining processes, setting design standards, and introducing research and experimentation at scale.

My work lives at the intersection of research and results. I partner closely with product, engineering, and marketing to ship experiences that don't just look good — they move the needle. I've driven $3M+ in revenue impact through improved conversion, led A/B testing programs with Optimizely, and increased visit-to-inquiry rates by over 50%.

When I'm not in Figma, you'll find me hiking, scuba diving, playing pickleball, or rewatching the Eras Tour.
